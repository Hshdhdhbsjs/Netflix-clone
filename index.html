  • Netflix is an entertainment company that offers a vast library of TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more through its streaming service. It began as a DVD rental service by mail back in 1997 but transitioned into streaming video on demand in 2007. Today, Netflix is known for producing original content—like hit series such as Stranger Things, The Witcher, and Bridgerton—in addition to hosting a wide array of licensed content from other networks and studios.

  • Canceling your Netflix subscription is pretty straightforward. Here are the steps: Log into Netflix: Go to Netflix.comand sign in to your account. Access Account Settings: Click on your profile icon in the top right corner and select "Account". Find Membership Details: Scroll down to the "Membership & Billing" section. Cancel Membership: Click on "Cancel Membership" and follow the prompts to confirm. You'll still be able to use Netflix until the end of your current billing period. If you have any issues or don't see the option to cancel, you might need to contact your payment provider for assistance1.
